C&ELA?

” C&ELA is a Swedish recording artist who first started singing in high school. He developed a passion for song writing while jotting down the lyrics to his favourite songs which he’d record straight from the radio onto his mother’s cassette tape. His first taste of music creation found him recording pre-written hooks for local artists, however this didn't give him the creative satisfaction he was after.

He managed to get in contact with Gabriel Silva through MySpace, who today is his main producer and close friend. Together they’ve managed to craft a sound that draws on real relationships and honest emotions.

C&ELA released his debut single “Bumper Cars” on the 29th of May 2020 and released his second single “Dim” on the 14th of August 2020 off his upcoming project Stereo Taped Monologues.”
